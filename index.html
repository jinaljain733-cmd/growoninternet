About us

A developer with an SEO brain

Grow on Internet is led by Jinal Jain, a Mumbai-based frontend developer and SEO specialist (SEO Analyst & Expert) with over 2 years of experience. The concept is simple: combine two critical skills that rarely live in the same person — building high-performance websites and ensuring Google can find them. I bridge this gap by handling responsive web development, semantic HTML, and Core Web Vitals on the build side, while driving technical, on-page, and local SEO, Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO), and App Store Optimisation (ASO) on the growth side. Every project is accelerated by AI-assisted workflows (ChatGPT, Claude, GitHub Copilot) for clients in Mumbai, across India, and internationally.
