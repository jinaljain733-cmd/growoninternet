Featured Client Project

Jinal Collection: From No Online Presence to Business Enquiries Within 7 Days

Mumbai · Fabric & Apparel Industry · Website Development & SEO

Jinal Collection is a Mumbai-based fabric and uniform supplier that previously had no website or digital presence. I designed and developed a fully responsive business website focused on user experience, lead generation, and search engine visibility. The project included a WhatsApp enquiry system, mobile-first design, technical SEO implementation, XML sitemap setup, robots.txt configuration, schema markup, Open Graph optimization, security enhancements, and deployment on a custom domain through Netlify. I also optimized the site for AI search (Answer Engine Optimization), structuring content and schema so the brand surfaces in Google AI Overviews — not just traditional search results. Result: Website launched in less than two weeks, with the first bulk-order enquiry received within just 7 days of going live.
